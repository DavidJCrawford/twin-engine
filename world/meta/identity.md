# Barrowford Vale (engine dev fixture)

Disposable generic fixture world (AR-4) — the engine's test rig, not a
shipped setting. Canon here originates from paper playtest session 01
(`playtest/session-01.md`).

- Calendar (TM-5): four seasons — Thaw, Greentide, Harvest, Frostfall;
  30 days each; dates written "N Season YYYY" (e.g. 1 Frostfall 1211).
  Moon: 30-day cycle; dark of the moon on the 2nd of Frostfall this year.
- Money: 12 pence (d) = 1 shilling (s). Taught diegetically before
  abbreviated (DESIGN §10).
- The layers (LR): mundane vale + a thin mythic layer under the rooted
  tradition [[the-old-rites]]. The gift of the twinned is called "the
  sight" here; it is regarded as odd, pitied, quietly consulted.
- Player character: [[wren]] (the sight since age nine).
