# Style bible — Barrowford Vale fixture (PR-2, DESIGN §8)

- Grounded, sensory, economical. Cold is specific (frost keeping its seat
  in shadows); money is exact; work has smells.
- Withhold: never narrate what Wren doesn't perceive; rarely everything
  they do. The uncanny arrives as one wrong detail in a mundane paragraph,
  never announced.
- People talk like their work: Berrin in short gates-closed sentences;
  Aldous in weighed clauses, courteous as a ledger.
- Similes come from the vale's own life (purses, gates, weather, wool) —
  nothing the vale has never seen.
- The rationalized version of any mythic event must be genuinely
  plausible; the villagers are not fools (LR-4 dignity rule).
