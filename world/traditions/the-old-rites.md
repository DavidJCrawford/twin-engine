# The Old Rites

What the vale kept before it forgot it was keeping anything: courtesies to
water, first-cut offerings, nods nobody explains. The Drowned Chapel was
their last house; its congregation is now a habit wearing thin. Under the
belief-economy coupling, what fades here is felt in the lake (LR-2).
